About

The bridge between
strategy and execution

I live in the gap between strategy and execution — and I’ve spent 15+ years building the infrastructure that closes it.

At heart a systems thinker and creative operator, I translate creative complexity into modular, scalable frameworks: from production workflows and digital style guides to AI-enabled content systems and governance structures.

A Cognitive Neuroscience background fuels my genuine curiosity about why teams work the way they do — and how to help creative minds do their best work within them.

Background

My Cognitive Neuroscience training taught me to look underneath behaviour to structure — past the symptom to the system. That orientation never left. It’s why I see what teams need before they can name it, and why the frameworks I build tend to hold.

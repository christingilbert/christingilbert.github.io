About

The bridge between
strategy and execution

I often work with marketing and creative teams navigating the integration of AI and digital production, helping them design production workflows, visual systems, and governance practices that allow creative work to scale while maintaining consistency, accessibility, and brand integrity.

As creative tools become more abundant, I'm increasingly interested in the curatorial side of the work, in shaping the digital environments and narratives that help people experience it in more meaningful ways.

Outside work, you will find me sailing through the Salish Sea, walking ancient pilgrimage routes or being supervised by my two furry kitties.

Mentorship · ADPList

Through ADPList I mentor designers and marketers across multiple countries, focusing on portfolio thinking, creative operations, and the growing role of AI in content production. I enjoy listening and helping creative minds recognize the structure of their own thinking - the patterns and decisions that make creative work sustainable over time.

Expertise

Three areas of deep practice

01 · Systems

Digital design frameworks

Visual style guides, component standards, asset specifications, and campaign structures help turn a brand's identity into practical production standards. They are made for designers, developers and marketers.

02 · AI

AI-enabled production

Modular generative AI workflows, structured prompt frameworks, and governance models that scale content production without compromising brand guidelines or responsible use.

03 · Operations

Creative operations

Production workflow design, cross-team alignment, content governance, and accessibility standards. This is the operational layer that makes creative teams efficiently, consistently and less dependent on any one person holding everything together.

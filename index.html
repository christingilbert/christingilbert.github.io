About

The bridge between
strategy and execution

For more than fifteen years I have been building the operational layer that helps creative work scale with integrity. Style guides, production frameworks, AI workflows, and governance structures: the systems that teams rely on to do their best work consistently and with confidence.

My background in Cognitive Neuroscience shapes how I approach this work. It fuels my genuine curiosity about why teams work the way they do, and how to design structures that help creative minds thrive within them.

Mentorship · ADPList

Through ADPList I mentor designers and marketers across multiple countries, focusing on portfolio thinking, creative operations, and the growing role of AI in content production. I enjoy listening and helping creative minds recognize the structure of their own thinking - the patterns and decisions that make creative work sustainable over time.
